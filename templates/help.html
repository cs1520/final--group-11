Help

Welcome to WhichWings, a website to help you decide which Big Shot Bob's wings you should order!

  • To start, login in or create an account.
  • If it's your first time here or if you'd like to reset your preferences, go to the Profile page and click "Take Survey". This will help us get to know your wing preferences better to help us pick out the best wings for you.
  • After the survey, you will be return to the Home page to view your results!
  • If you'd like to search any specific wing names or flavors, head over to our Search page.
